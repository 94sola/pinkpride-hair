about
PinkPride

Our beauty salon was founded in 1990, and since then we are trying to make our customers standout and confident!

At Pinkpride, we believe in the transformative power of self-expression through hair and cosmetics. Whether you're looking to unleash your inner diva with vibrant hair colors, seeking the perfect skincare routine, or in pursuit of makeup that speaks volumes, our diverse range of products and services caters to all your beauty needs.

our passion

At PinkPride, we are passionate about offering the best selection of hair cosmetics and makeup kits. Our commitment to quality ingredients is unmatched. We believe that our customers deserve the very best, and that's why we only use the finest ingredients in our products. Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional service and expertise to ensure that you find the perfect products for your unique needs. We are proud to be the go-to destination for all your hair and cosmetic needs.

Our mission

At PinkPride, we believe that beauty is more than just your hair. Our mission is to help you bring out your inner beauty and confidence with our high-quality hair extensions and glamorous makeup kits. We are passionate about empowering women to feel their best, all day long.
